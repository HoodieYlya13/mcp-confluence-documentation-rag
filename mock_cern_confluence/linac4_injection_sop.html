Standard Operating Procedure

This is the standard procedure for managing the injection sequence of H- ion beams from Linac4 into the Proton Synchrotron Booster (PSB).

Linac4 H- Ion Beam Injection Sequence SOP

Linac4 accelerates H- ions to an energy of 160 MeV. The injection into the PSB uses a charge-exchange system with a carbon stripping foil to convert H- ions to protons (H+).

1. Beam Sequence States

The sequence must proceed through the following operational phases:

Phase State Name Target Intensity (charges) RF Cavity Phase (deg) Interlock Status
1 BEAM_OFF 0.0 0.0 ARMD
2 PILOT_BEAM 5.0e11 -45.2 ARMD
3 PRODUCTION_BEAM 2.5e12 -12.8 ACTIVE

2. Stripping Foil Verification Checklist

  • Verify the stripping foil position using the BTV (Beam Television) monitor.
  • Check the stripping foil temperature. If it exceeds 1800K, command the foil changer to switch to a spare foil position.
  • Ensure the current in the bumper magnets matches the injection orbit offsets within ±0.5 mm.

The stripping foil has a finite lifetime. Track the cumulative integrated charge on the active foil (position 4) and flag when it reaches 1.0e20 protons.
